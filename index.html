My Ham Radio Journey

Hello fellow hams! I'm Jagadeesh N Malakannavar, an amateur radio operator from India with the callsign VU3JNM. I got licensed in 2015 and have been actively participating in various amateur radio activities since then.

My primary interests include HF communications, antenna design, and participating in contests. I'm also passionate about promoting amateur radio among youth and supporting emergency communication networks.

When I'm not on the air, you can find me tinkering with electronics, building antennas, or helping newcomers get started in this wonderful hobby.

DXing

Actively working DX stations from around the world. Always looking for new countries to add to my log.

Homebrew

Enjoy building my own equipment and antennas. Currently working on a multi-band HF vertical.

Emergency Comms

Trained in emergency communications. Ready to serve when needed.

How to QSL with VU3JNM

I confirm all QSOs and would be happy to exchange QSL cards. Here are the different ways you can get a confirmation from me:

Direct Mail

Send your QSL card with sufficient return postage or IRC to my address. I will reply with my QSL card.

LoTW

I actively use Logbook of The World. Upload your logs to LoTW for quick electronic confirmation.

eQSL

I accept eQSL confirmations. My eQSL.cc account is active and I confirm all valid QSOs.

Bureau

I participate in the incoming and outgoing QSL bureaus. Cards are processed through the Indian QSL bureau.
